Owe Dam, Kharghar

A personal haven for nature lovers

Owe Dam is located in the forest area of Owe Village (also Owe Camp) in Kharghar, Navi Mumbai. It is unofficially one of the best picnic locations in Kharghar, though in an isolated spot nestled between the Kharghar Hills and Owe Hills. For those who are unaware and have heard the name “Owe” for the first time; Owe is a village in Owe Camp area, Kharghar, Navi Mumbai. The Owe Dam lies in the interiors, towards the forest area of Kharghar (Owe) Hills near Gholwadi village, and is constructed on a holding point or Owe lake, as people call it, which is located right in the centre of the hills that cover the water body like a fence. For first timers, the Owe Dam is not a huge dam or an exploited picnic spot like Pandavkada (Kharghar) Waterfalls (which is prohibited) or the driving range waterfalls (which is highly crowded and exploited). The aloofness and calmness of the Owe Dam area makes it the best of the lot and it is the ideal choice for those who like to enjoy the company of nature alone, and not amid reckless and unconcerned frolickers.

For those who like to simply jump and splash in water, the Owe Dam is a safe water body unless you act irresponsibly.

How to reach Owe Dam: Best mode of transportation

You can reach Owe Dam, Kharghar, by Train, Bus, Four/Two Wheeler, Cycle or on Foot/Walking
Walking: This one is the best mode for locals and even visitors to enjoy the adventurous walk to the Owe Dam.
Cycle: Cyclists can pedal all the way to Owe Dam cycling to the dam would be a great experience as it is easy to manage that leads to the dam.
Train: The Kharghar Railway station is the nearest railway station to reach Owe Dam. From Kharghar station you can take an auto or a sharing ride.
Bus: There are multiple buses that start from the Kharghar Railway station that run on the Kharghar bus route that passes along the Owe Village bus stop
Four Wheeler and Two Wheeler: From Utsav Chowk, drive all along the Raintree Marg, past TATA Hospital. From Taloja, drive all the way towards Hospital.
